JobsForHer Foundation, a Charitable Trust launched in 2018, is dedicated to enhancing the status of women in the workplace and beyond. Located in Bengaluru, the foundation is dedicated to promoting positive transformation and ensuring equal opportunities for women throughout India.

Our Vision and Mission

With a keen understanding of the importance of enabling women, the trust is on a mission to foster their career advancement and beyond. This involves unlocking their untapped potential and enhancing self-confidence through initiatives in education, skill development, and facilitating connections with various opportunities on HerKey.

Our key focus areas include:

  • Promoting gender equality and empowering women
  • Providing accessible learning opportunities
  • Fostering professional development
  • Building supportive communities

The Team

Neha Bagaria

Managing Trustee, JobsForHer Foundation

Neha Bagaria is the Managing Trustee at JobsForHer Foundation – a Charitable Trust launched in 2018, dedicated to enhancing the status of women in the workplace and beyond.

As the Founder & CEO of HerKey (formerly JobsForHer) – India's largest career engagement platform for women, she has been instrumental in unlocking opportunities, learning, and communities for women by connecting them with career champions since 2015.

A Wharton graduate, Neha's impressive career includes founding Paragon, an educational company that brought the Advanced Placement Program to India, and working at Kemwell, a biotech company, in HR, Finance, and Marketing strategy.

Her achievements have earned her recognition in Forbes India's WPower Trailblazers list and the BusinessLine Changemaker Awards 2023 for Social Transformation in India.

Based in Bangalore and a mother to two athletic boys, Neha is an avid traveller and adventure sports enthusiast.

Ashok Pamidi

Project Head – herShakti

Ashok is a 30-year industry veteran with extensive leadership experience. In his most recent role, he served as the CEO of NASSCOM Foundation.

His impressive career includes positions as:

  • Geo Expansion Leader at IBM
  • Transformation Leader at HP (Hewlett Packard)

Throughout his career, Ashok has been involved as a strategic thought leader with various forums worldwide. He currently serves as an advisory board member for start-ups and as guest faculty for prominent Indian institutions.
